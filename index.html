about

Curiosity
as a discipline.

I'm Vahini — a pseudonym for a teenager in the UK who is deeply, genuinely obsessed with physics and what the universe is actually made of.

I started by reading everything I could find about black holes, then wrote a paper about the information paradox. Now I want to build things — telescopes, spectrometers, simulations — to get even closer to the questions that keep me up at night.

I also take photos. The two feel connected — both are about looking carefully at things and trying to understand what you're seeing.

Vahini is an alias. All writing and projects are original work.
